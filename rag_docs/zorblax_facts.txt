The Zorblax is a fictional creature invented for testing.
Key facts about the Zorblax:
- It has exactly 7 legs and bright purple feathers.
- It lives only in the Glimmering Caves of Aethelgard.
- Its favorite food is crystallized moonberries.
- The secret password used by Zorblax wranglers is "QUASAR-9921".
- Zorblax can hold their breath for 42 minutes underwater.
